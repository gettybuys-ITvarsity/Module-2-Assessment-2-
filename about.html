About

Pine City Mall is a 69,000 m² shopping mall situated alongside the N2 freeway near the towns of Somerset West and Strand, in the Western Cape province of South Africa. The mall comprises 200 stores and 3,955 parking spaces. Its anchors include Woolworths, Pick n Pay, Game, and Edgar.pine City Mall has 200 stores offering an inviting and engaging lifestyle shopping experience, including a wide selection of speciality retailers. Pine City Mall’s restaurants, cafés and coffee shops, serve a superb selection of everyday staples and delicacies. In addition to its popularity with the Western Cape’s trendsetting shoppers, Somerset Mall is also a must-visit tourist destination. It attracts thousands of international visitors every year with its singular array of local and international retail brands, inviting ambience and fun, festive events.
